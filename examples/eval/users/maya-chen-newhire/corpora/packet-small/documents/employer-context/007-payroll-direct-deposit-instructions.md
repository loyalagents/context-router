# Direct Deposit Setup Notes

Pacific Ledger Cooperative employees enter deposit details in LedgerPay after
the HR profile is created. The payroll portal asks for the account holder name,
financial institution name, account type, routing value, and account value.
The portal also keeps the employee mailing address beside the deposit profile
so payroll reviewers can compare it with tax setup records.

For official payment setup forms, payroll staff should copy only values from the
employee's own confirmed deposit profile. Blank sample packets and help center
articles are not source records for a specific employee. Signature and
certification areas remain manual steps handled outside the stored-memory flow.
