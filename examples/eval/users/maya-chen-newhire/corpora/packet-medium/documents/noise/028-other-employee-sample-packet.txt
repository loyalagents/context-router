SECTION 2: EMPLOYMENT ELIGIBILITY (FORM I-9)
--------------------------------------------------
All new employees are required to complete Form I-9 to verify their identity and authorization to work in the United States.

Instructions:
1. On or before your first day, you will receive a task to complete Section 1 of the Form I-9 electronically.
2. Within your first three days of employment, you must present original, unexpired documents to an HR representative to complete Section 2.
3. A list of acceptable documents will be provided with the electronic form. Common examples include a U.S. Passport OR a combination of a Driver's License and Social Security Card.

Please prepare to bring your chosen documents with you on your first day.

SECTION 4: DIRECT DEPOSIT AUTHORIZATION
--------------------------------------------------
We encourage all employees to enroll in direct deposit for secure and timely payment. This is also managed within the LedgerPay payroll system.

To set up direct deposit, you will need the following information for your bank account(s):
- Bank Name
- Bank Routing Number (9 digits)
- Bank Account Number
- Account Type (Checking or Savings)

You can split your deposit between multiple accounts.

Sample Setup in LedgerPay:
