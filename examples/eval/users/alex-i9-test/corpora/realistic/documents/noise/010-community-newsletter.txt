OAKWOOD HILLS COMMUNITY NEWSLETTER - AUTUMN EDITION

Get Ready for the Annual Fall Festival!

Join your neighbors on Saturday, October 21st, for our much-anticipated annual Fall Festival at Meadowbrook Park. The fun starts at 11:00 AM and goes until 4:00 PM.

This year's festival will feature live music from local band "The Wandering Pines," a pumpkin carving contest (bring your own pumpkin!), and a bake sale to support the community garden project. We'll also have a variety of food trucks on-site, including a new addition serving delicious apple cider donuts.

Event Schedule:
11:00 AM - Festival Opens
12:00 PM - Live Music by The Wandering Pines
2:00 PM - Pumpkin Carving Contest Judging
3:00 PM - Bake Sale Last Call
4:00 PM - Festival Closes

This is a free event for all residents. We are still looking for a few volunteers to help with setup and cleanup. If you're interested in lending a hand for an hour or two, please reach out to the events committee. Your help is greatly appreciated and makes these events possible!

We look forward to seeing you all there for a day of community fun
