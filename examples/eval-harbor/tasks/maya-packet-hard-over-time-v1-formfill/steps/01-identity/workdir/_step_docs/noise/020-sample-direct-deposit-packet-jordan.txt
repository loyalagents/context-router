SECTION 2: INSTRUCTIONS & OVERVIEW (SAMPLE)
------------------------------------------------------------------
This packet demonstrates the process for setting up direct deposit in the LedgerPay system. It shows the information required and the format of the confirmation you will receive after submission.

To set up your own direct deposit, please navigate to the "Payment Elections" section of your employee profile. You will need the following information for each account:

- Financial Institution Name
- Account Category (as listed in the payroll dropdown)
- ABA Routing Number (a 9-digit number)
- Bank Account Number

You can add up to three (3) accounts for deposit. You can choose to deposit a specific flat amount or a percentage of your net pay into each account. One account must be designated to receive the remainder of your pay.

The following sections contain a completed example for a sample employee.
